Biography Timeline


Born June 6, 1907 Frieda Kahlo Calderon is born as third daughter of Matilde Calderon de Kahlo, Mexican, and Wilhelm Kahlo, German, in Coyoacan, a suburb of Mexico City.
1913 Contacted Polio: Frida Kahlo contracts polio, which leaves her with a slight crooked right foot. She attends the Colegio Aleman elementary school in Mexico City.
1922 Enter College: Frida Kahlo attends the Esecuela Nacional Preparatoria with a view to studying medicine at university. She admires Diego Rivera at work at her school on fresco "The Creation".
1925: Traffic Accident. Frida Kahlo is seriously injurd in a collision between a tram and the bus in which she and her friend traveling home from school.
1928: Meet Diego Rivera. Frida Kahlo became a member of the Mexican Communist Party and meets Diego Rivera again. They fell in love.
1929: Married Diego Rivera.
1930: First Miscarriage.
1932: Move to Detroit. In April the couple move to Detroit, where Rivera has been awarded another commission. After threee and a half months, Frida Kahlo`s second pregnancy ends 4 July with a miscarriage at the Henry Ford Hospital.
1933: Move to New York in March.Return to Mexico in December into a new house in San Angel.
1934: Miscarriage. Diego has an affair with her younger sister, Cristina.
1935: Seperated with Diego. She meets the American sculptor Isamu Noguchi and has an affair with him. She travels to New York with a number of women friends.
1936: Active in Solidarity Commitee.
1937: Welcomed Leon Trotsky and Natalia Sedova arrive in Mexico. Frida Kahlo places the Blue Housein Coyoacan at their disposal.
1938: Frist Solo Exhibiton. In Julien Levy`s gallery in New York. It is a great success. She begins an affair with the photographer Nickolas Muray.
1929: Travelled to Paris. Exhibits her works in the Renou & Clle gallery .
1940: Remarried with Diego.
1941: Father Passed Away.
1942: Joined Seminario de Culture Mexicana. Starts to write diary.
1943: Professor at La Esmeralda School of Art. After only a few monts she is forced by poor health to hold her classes at homw in Coyoacan.
1944: She receives a national prize from the Ministry of Public Education for her picture Moses. She goesto New York for an operation on her spine.
1948: rejoins the Mexican Communist Party.
1949: She undergoes a total of seven operation on her spine and spends nine months in hospital.
1950: Poor Health Condition.
1951: Supported Mexico`s Peace Movement.
1953: Art Exhibition by Lola Alvarez Bravo. Frida attends the opening lying in bed.
1954: Passed Away. She catches pneumonia, and against of her doctors advice she takes part in a demonstration against North-American intervention in Guatermala. On 13 July Frida Kahlo dies in the Blue House.
